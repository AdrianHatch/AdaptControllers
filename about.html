Who we are

Welcome to adapt

We are the company that puts the power back into the hands of gamers. We are a team of passionate gamers and tech enthusiasts who have a deep love for gaming and the gaming community. Our goal is to create the ultimate gaming experience for every gamer out there.

We believe that every gamer should be able to customize their gaming experience to fit their unique playstyle. That's why we have created a range of customizable gaming controllers for Xbox, Playstation, and PC. Our controllers are designed to give gamers the ability to fully customize their controller to their liking, so they can game with comfort, style, and precision.

At Adapt, we are committed to quality and excellence. Our controllers are made with the highest quality materials and are built to last. We work with the best designers and engineers in the industry to ensure that our controllers are not only functional but also look great. From the buttons to the thumbsticks, every aspect of our controllers is designed with the gamer in mind.

But we don't just stop at customization. We are constantly innovating and pushing the boundaries of what is possible in gaming. Our team of experts is always researching and testing new technologies to integrate into our controllers, so you can have the most advanced gaming experience possible.

We are proud to be a part of the gaming community, and we are committed to supporting gamers in any way we can. Our mission is to empower gamers and give them the tools they need to take their gaming to the next level. We are Adapt, and we are here to give power back to the gamer.

Innovation

At Adapt, we've found a better way to play. To produce a better, more social gaming experience, we've pushed the limits of creativity. By making use of a player's previously unutilized fingers on the back of the controller, we have improved the dexterity of their hand. We've been able to speed up response times and boost performance by positioning remappable paddles where your fingers normally rest because players' thumbs never leave the thumbsticks. We won't stop there, even if our inventions have generated over 161 patents (granted and pending). Since we think that "better" may always be enhanced.
